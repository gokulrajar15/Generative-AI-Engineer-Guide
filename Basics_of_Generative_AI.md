## 13. Understanding LLM benchmarks
- **13.1 GPQA PhD Science Expertise**: 448 expert questions; non-PhD humans score 34% even with web access.
- **13.2 MMLU-PRO Language understanding**: A more advanced and cleaned-up version of Massive Multitask Language Understanding including choice of 10 instead of 4.
- **13.3 AIME Math**: Hard competitive Math puzzles from the prestigious, invite-only Math competition for top high schoolers.
- **13.4 LiveCode Bench Coding**: Holistic benchmark for evaluating Code LLMs based on problems from contests on LeetCode, AtCoder, and Codeforces.
- **13.5 MuSR Reasoning**: Logical deduction, such as analyzing 1,000-word murder mystery and answering: “Who has means, motive, and opportunity?”
- **13.6 HLE Super-human intelligence**: 2,500 of the toughest, subject-diverse, multi-modal questions designed to be the last academic exam of its kind for AI.
